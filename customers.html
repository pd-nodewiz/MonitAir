Who We Serve

Customer segments with clear buying triggers

MonitAir messaging is built around operational outcomes each buyer actually cares about: adherence lift, intervention speed, staff efficiency, and program scalability.

Sleep Centers and Enterprise Sleep Networks

Buyer concern: first-90-day PAP drop-off and patient follow-through.

  • Early adherence risk stratification
  • Worklists for proactive patient outreach
  • Program visibility across clinics and staff

Pulmonary Practices and Respiratory Specialists

Buyer concern: decompensation risk and late intervention patterns.

  • NIV and chronic respiratory risk monitoring
  • Prioritized escalation queues by severity
  • Integrated remote follow-up workflows

DME and Home Respiratory Organizations

Buyer concern: setup-to-compliance leakage and retention pressure.

  • Setup, adherence, and resupply workflow alignment
  • Actionable queues for patient support teams
  • Cleaner performance tracking for operations leaders

Near-term Customer Universe

Quantifiable starting points

  • More than 2,300 AASM-accredited sleep centers in the U.S. (AASM)
  • About 4,600 practices routinely billed Medicare RPM in 2024 (HHS OIG)
  • Nearly 1 million Medicare enrollees received RPM in 2024 (HHS OIG)

Positioning Language That Converts

For clinical buyers: identify risk sooner, intervene with confidence, and keep patients engaged in therapy.

For operational buyers: scale patient oversight without linearly scaling headcount.

For financial buyers: protect high-value chronic care programs from avoidable adherence and intervention failures.
