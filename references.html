Data Inputs Used Across This Marketing Site

Figures below support market context, customer sizing, and prioritization logic used in the MonitAir static experience.

Sleep Apnea Prevalence Baseline

Benjafield AV, et al. Lancet Respiratory Medicine (2019). Literature-based estimate: 936 million adults globally with OSA; 54 million in the U.S.

Potentially Higher U.S. OSA Burden

Malhotra A, et al. Value in Health (published Jan 2025). Literature review estimating up to 83.7 million U.S. adults with OSA.

COPD U.S. Burden

CDC National Center for Health Statistics (updated May 15, 2024): nearly 16 million U.S. adults reported having COPD in 2023, with prevalence around 3.8%.

RPM Adoption and Reimbursement Momentum

HHS OIG Data Snapshot (August 2025): nearly 1 million Medicare enrollees received RPM in 2024, Medicare paid approximately $536 million, and about 4,600 practices routinely billed RPM.

Economic Burden of Undiagnosed OSA

American Academy of Sleep Medicine (2016): estimated annual U.S. economic burden of undiagnosed sleep apnea at $149.6 billion.

PAP Adherence Challenge

Weaver et al., UpToDate (2020); Truong et al., Journal of Clinical Sleep Medicine (2018): non-adherence commonly approximated around 50% in PAP populations.

Sleep Center Universe

American Academy of Sleep Medicine public materials: more than 2,300 AASM-accredited sleep centers in the U.S.

Modeling Notes and Assumptions

  • Condition pools are shown as non-deduplicated unless noted.
  • Serviceable cohort scenarios (5%/10%/15%) are planning assumptions.
  • Market pages are for strategic sizing, not clinical or reimbursement guarantees.
